Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Proselenos is a writing application that respects your privacy and gives you complete control over your data. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Core Promise

Your manuscripts and writing projects are stored exclusively in your own Google Drive account. We do not store copies of your creative work on our servers.

1. Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, we request permission to:

  • View your basic profile information: Your name and email address for authentication purposes
  • Access your Google Drive: To create, read, and modify writing projects stored in your Drive
  • Access Google Docs: To create and edit documents for your manuscripts

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your Google Account security settings.

2. How Your Data is Stored

Your Google Drive is your storage: All your writing projects, manuscripts, and related files are stored in a dedicated "proselenos_projects" folder in your Google Drive. This means:

  • You maintain complete ownership of your content
  • Your manuscripts are accessible to you even outside our app
  • Deleting files from your Drive removes them from the app
  • We cannot access your content if you revoke our permissions

3. Configuration and Settings

The app stores two types of configuration files in your Drive:

  • General settings: Your preferences like selected AI model, author name, and current project
  • Sensitive settings: API keys for AI services are encrypted before storage using industry-standard encryption (AES-256)

4. AI Services and API Keys

If you choose to use AI-powered features:

  • You provide your own API keys for services like OpenAI or Anthropic
  • These keys are encrypted and stored in your Google Drive
  • Only our servers can decrypt these keys when you request AI assistance
  • We never store your API keys on our servers
  • Chat conversations with AI can be saved to your Drive at your discretion

5. Data Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data like API keys are encrypted before storage
  • Server-side operations: All Drive and AI interactions happen on our secure servers, not in your browser
  • Minimal access: We only request the Google permissions necessary for the app to function
  • Token management: Authentication tokens are handled securely and refreshed automatically

6. What We Don't Do

  • We don't store your manuscripts on our servers
  • We don't sell or share your personal information
  • We don't use your content to train AI models
  • We don't access your Drive content for any purpose other than app functionality
  • We don't track your writing or analyze your content

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your files directly in Google Drive
  • Portability: Your manuscripts are in standard formats you can use anywhere
  • Deletion: Remove files from Drive to delete them from the app
  • Revocation: Remove app access through Google Account settings at any time

8. Browser Storage

We use minimal browser storage only for:

  • Remembering your preferred text-to-speech voice setting
  • This contains no personal information or Drive content

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy when we make changes.
